Baseball Tools

Profile Card BatTrax

This tool allows you to track game pitches similar to other software (BATS/Trackman). You set your rosters & lineups, then log each pitch (pitch type, result of the pitch/ball in play, baserunners, etc. This is a lightweight game/pitch tracker without video or ball metrics functionality.

 Intended Zone Pitch Tracker

This tool allows you to track a pitcher's control and command! You simply click on the strike zone where they are targeting to throw their pitch, and then click the plot once more to show where the pitch ended up.
